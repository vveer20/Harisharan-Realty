Our Story

About Harisharan Realty

Founded on the vision of Late Architect Shri Harish M. Thakkar, a pioneer known for his design excellence, integrity, and deep commitment to society, the Harisharan Group carries forward a legacy of thoughtful design, integrity, and community service.

Today, the Group is led by Mr. Nalin Thakkar, a civil engineer, steering design excellence with an in-house team of professionals across architecture, engineering, and project management.
